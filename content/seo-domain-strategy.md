# SEO domain strategy: nordicemobility.se + elscooterservice.se

Context: `elscooterservice.se` is owned by the same business, so it should be treated as an owned search asset, not a competitor.

## Recommended direction

Use `nordicemobility.se` as the main brand, booking and commerce domain.

Use `elscooterservice.se` as one of these:

1. **Satellite service site** for high-intent local searches such as "elscooter service Orebro", "punktering elscooter" and "elsparkcykel verkstad".
2. **Redirect asset** if the goal is to consolidate all authority into Nordic E-Mobility.

## Best near-term setup

Keep `elscooterservice.se` live as a tightly focused service site, but make Nordic E-Mobility the obvious operator.

The site should:

- Link prominently to `nordicemobility.se/book-online/`
- Use consistent NAP: Nordic E-Mobility AB, Pistolvagen 6, Orebro, 070-024 33 19
- Avoid duplicate copy from Nordic pages
- Target narrow searches Nordic does not fully own yet
- Include local service pages that point users to Nordic booking

## When to 301 redirect

Use 301 redirects if:

- The site has little organic traffic
- It has thin/old content that competes with Nordic
- You want one strong brand instead of two properties

Suggested redirect mapping:

- `elscooterservice.se/priser/` -> `nordicemobility.se/elscooter-reparation-orebro/`
- `elscooterservice.se/punktering/` -> `nordicemobility.se/punktering-elscooter-orebro/`
- `elscooterservice.se/batteri/` -> `nordicemobility.se/batterireparation-elscooter/`
- `elscooterservice.se/kontakt/` -> `nordicemobility.se/book-online/`

## Content angle for elscooterservice.se

Keep it plain and search-focused:

- "Elscooter service Orebro"
- "Punktering elscooter Orebro"
- "Elsparkcykel verkstad Orebro"
- "Dackbyte elscooter"

Let Nordic carry the premium brand, battery lab, product sales and broader e-mobility positioning.
