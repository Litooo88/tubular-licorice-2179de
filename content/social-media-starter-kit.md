# Social media starter kit for Nordic E-Mobility

Purpose: turn workshop photos and short phone clips into trustworthy, local content for Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, Google Business Profile and the website.

## Folder structure

Use this structure when you share the image dump:

- `content/inbox/raw-workshop/` for every original file
- `content/inbox/raw-products/` for product and showroom photos
- `content/inbox/raw-before-after/` for repair cases
- `content/selected/workshop/` for strong workshop photos
- `content/selected/before-after/` for approved case photos
- `content/selected/social/` for cropped social exports
- `content/selected/website/` for hero, service and trust photos

## Photo selection rules

Pick images that show real trust signals:

- Hands working on a scooter, battery, wheel, brake or controller
- Clean bench, tools, meters, labels and organized parts
- Before/after repair results
- Customer-safe details: no visible private customer data, plates, invoices or faces without approval
- Clear local proof: workshop, signage, branded clothing, van, address context

Avoid images that show:

- Unsafe battery handling
- Messy benches where the main subject is unclear
- Customer information, order numbers or personal data
- Products that look damaged unless used in a clear repair story

## Weekly content pillars

1. Repair proof: one real fault, one diagnosis, one fix.
2. Battery lab: testing, safety, range, winter storage.
3. Local trust: inside the workshop, pick-up, deliveries, team.
4. Product clarity: legal commuter vs private-mark performance model.
5. Education: rules, care tips, buying used, warning signs.

## Caption formulas

Repair case:
`Problem: [symptom]. Diagnosis: [cause]. Fix: [action]. Result: [customer outcome]. Book a diagnosis before buying random parts.`

Battery tip:
`Battery tip: [one habit]. Why it matters: [simple reason]. Warning sign: [symptom]. Need a battery test in Orebro? Book Nordic E-Mobility.`

Product trust:
`Thinking about [model/type]? We help you understand range, service, warranty and where the scooter is actually meant to be used.`
